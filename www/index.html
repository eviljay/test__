Overview
Player Reviews

Starburst is the simplest game with awesome 96.10% RTP. Made in 3D, its graphics do not bring any stress to the eyes. Even sparkling gems icons look very harmonically. Their combinations bring small awards, but the gamblers win very often.

Overview

NetEnt has created this easy slot for those ones, who simply gamble to rest, but never forget that even the relaxation can bring good money. The smallest stake here is 1 coin, and the gambler can pick the number of lines (10 of them is the maximum). The largest bet is 6. The special jackpots absence does not decrease the players’ interest to Starburst.

Extra Features

When you rotate the reels here, you are watching the sky and the stars. Gems look bright — several of their images in a certain combination give you a prize. The largest sum you can get here reaches 50 thousand! The probability of a win here is pretty high — it reaches 23%. The largest payout (these results come from the tests) is the bet multiplied 438. The Wild that can be expanded enlarges the gambler’s chances of getting more money.
